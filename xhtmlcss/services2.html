Efficiency Architect Engagement

The Efficiency Architect Engagement (hereinafter “Engagement”) is the flagship service offered by A.G.F.M. It is a holistic consulting service aimed at providing global efficiency solutions to small and mid-size enterprises. The Engagement focuses on maximizing enterprise profitability and sustainability.

Through an initial Pre-Engagement Interview (“Interview”), and subsequent completion of the proprietary Efficiency Architect Survey (“Survey”) by the client, the Architects provide a customized Efficiency Architect Analysis (“Analysis”) detailing specific administrative and operational efficiency solutions.

The Analysis is a multiple-page roadmap aimed to deliver specific and actionable solutions for the client, without overwhelming management with non-value added recommendations. It focuses on the Core Pillars of Business Sustainability (“Core Pillars”) developed by the Architects.

Once the Analysis is completed, and an Efficiency Architect Consultation (“Consultation”) is conducted with the client to assure full comprehension, the Architects perform subsequent recurring meetings to aid in the implementation of the solutions.



Venture Capital

In 2011, A.G.F.M. officially entered the world of Venture Capital. Through its vast network of angel investors and other business leaders, A.G.F.M. and its affiliates have access to an undisclosed amount of capital available as seed money for innovative and ground-breaking ideas and businesses. By utilizing its substantial operational and administrative expertise, A.G.F.M. delivers both capital advancement and seasoned management knowledge in order to nurture an idea into a business or rapidly accelerate an existing business to new heights.

A.G.F.M. leverages its Efficiency Architect Engagement (refer to “Efficiency Architect Engagement” page) as a conduit to assess whether existing and/or potential clients are capable of exponential and explosive growth through further capital infusion. If so, A.G.F.M. may choose to be that source of capital to carry a business idea into a business empire.
